The Great Dalmuti

Chad drew a Queen, so his game was first. He decided we'd play two rounds, and each player would score points for their team given their fininishing position. Zero points for Greater Peon, one for Lesser Peon, 2-5 for the Merchants, seven for Lesser Dalmuti and nine for Great Dalmuti. Each team could only have four people play. We randomly picked original starting positions: Steph as Greater Dalmuti, Captain as Lesser Dalmuti, Lorelei, Uncle Giant, Grammy and James as Merchants, Cat as Lesser Peon and Chad as Greater Peon. After the first round: Captian is Great Dalmuti, Uncle Giant is Lesser Dalmuti, Lorelei is first Merchant, Chad is second, Grammy is third and Steph is fourth Merchant. Cat was Lesser Peon and James Greater Peon. Captain, Giant, Chad, Grammy, Steph, James, Lore, Cat. Score for the first round was 20-11, second round was 23-8; for a total of 43-19.
