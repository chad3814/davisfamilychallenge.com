Sixth Annual Davis Family Challenge

Candy Cane Ruldolphs

The Tails' teamwork narrowly defeated the Heads. They completed their 12 Rudulphs first.

Are You Smarter Than a Fifth Grader

After three rounds of five questions each, both teams were tied at 14. We did a shoot-out and the Tails got their question and the Heads failed.

Magnetic Rocket Toss

The Heads thoroughly destroyed the Tails 13-9.

House of Cards

High on their Magnetic Rocket Toss victory, the Heads were the first team to think of bending the cards in half to make them stand. They got their cards into a single structure first.

Hong Kong

The Tails took a commanding lead winning the first two games in a best of five match. The Heads tried to get something started with a W in the third game, but the Tails rallied for the win in the fourth game.
