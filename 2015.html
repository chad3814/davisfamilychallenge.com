Steph's game was first, and she started out strong, scoring in the first nine rounds, and dominating the early game. We decided before play started to go to 8,000 instead of 10,000. I Spy

Grammy's game of I Spy, was played with a revolving Christmas Tree. Players drew a card that had a picture of a random ornament on it and they were timed to find the ornament on the tree. Team Eddie had a big lead that Team Steph was unable to overcome. Team Eddie won 22:07 to Team Steph's 22:51 (despite the late argument about Uncle Giant's reach around).

King of Tokyo

Eddie's Game was King of Tokyo. Each team fielded a duo controlling a monster. Eddie/Uncle Giant, Chad/Captain, J.D./Grammy, Cat/Lorelei. Eddie & Uncle Giant got the victory, giving Team Eddie a three-game sweep! The other duos were all close in experience and health.
